The Black hole Accretion and High Energy Astrophysics Group is lead by Prof. Feng Yuan and currently hosts three additional faculty members, three postdocs and eight graduate students.

Our current research topics include:

  • Black hole accretion, jet formation, and wind
  • Active galactic nuclei
  • AGNs feedback and galaxy evolution
  • Black hole X-ray binaries
  • Planet formation

For any questions or comments regarding this website, please contact us.
